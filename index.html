CHEVALIER T THOMAS EDUCATIONAL TRUST.

(A Public Charitable Trust (Regd.))

The school is run under Chevalier T Thomas Educational Trust (Regd.), a public Charitable Trust, started by Chev. T Thomas the Founder. The Trust is a leading Educational agency which already has in its fold four recognized leading institutions in Chennai – a Matric Boys’ Hr. Sec. School and a Matric Girls’ Hr. Sec. School in PERAMBUR, Chennai, a Matric Hr. Sec. School (Co-educational) at Redhills and a women’s college in Perambur. The three schools are affiliated to the Matriculation and Hr. Sec. Boards of Govt. of Tamilnadu and the college is affiliated to the University of Madras. Marian Sr. Sec. School is the only school in Kerala under the Management which is affiliated to the Central Board of sec. Education, New Delhi.

The object of the Trust is to establish and manage Educational institutions of high standard and to serve the society and nation by helping the needy and deserving students irrespective of Caste, Community, Creed or Religion.

The Governing Body consists of eminent citizens and educationists.
